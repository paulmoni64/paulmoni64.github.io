MY AUTOBIOGRAPHY

My name is Paul Nganga Moni, 21 years of age. I am the first born in a family of three;

Gilgil town, Nakuru County is where I hail and is my birthplace. Life hs had alot to give since the year 1997 upto date. Opportunities, ups and downs been part of my life. Having joined my kindergaten at
Down Ways Academy at Gilgil and later joined Kanyua Primary School where i did my KCSE exam and scored 380 marks. This wasn't as bad since I qualified for my next level of

Dedan Kimathi University Of Technology to persue a Bachelor of Business Information Technology. Currently am in third year of my study.
